Distribution · the AI hook video

The video is the trigger.

AI generates a 9–16 vertical clip for every game: the satisfying opening, a near-miss or payoff, then "play now." Ship to YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok. The clip fires Circuits 01 & 02; the site closes the loop with 05, 06, 08.

01

Hook — first 3 seconds.

AI renders the game's most satisfying opening frame and motion. The brain decides to keep watching before the second tick.

02

Tease — the near-miss.

A play that almost succeeds (or a payoff just out of reach). Circuit 01 fires on the uncertainty; Circuit 06 leaves the loop open.

03

CTA — "play now."

The clip resolves only by clicking through. The site is the action (Circuit 05, near-zero friction); the game is the variable reward (Circuit 01).

04

Measure — per-clip.

CTR clip→site, play rate, session length, D1/D7. Clips that don't convert are re-cut or killed; the next batch inherits what worked.
