## Funny-face payoff
The player starts rapid-fire blinking to clear every obstacle — the webcam catches their eyes going haywire like a broken robot. The harder the obstacles, the more frantic the twitching. Perfect 5-second TikTok/Reels reaction clip.

## Control
- **Trigger:** eyeBlinkLeft + eyeBlinkRight (>0.45 threshold)
- **Action:** hop (single jump, ground-only)
- **Power:** blink intensity scales jump height

## Obstacle design
- Neon-red pillars of varying height (short or tall)
- Scrolling right-to-left
- Speed increases feel via frame rate
