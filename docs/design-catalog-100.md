# Design Catalog — 100 games on conditioned neural paths

> Generated from engine/state/design-backlog.json (2026-07-17). Each design hijacks a
> specific IG/TikTok-installed habit. Feed the loop: pick a design, add a trend +

## Feed & Gesture Mechanics (25)

1. **Pull to Pop** (pull-to-pop, viral) — _pull-to-refresh lever_ · Drag the feed down like a pull-to-refresh, then release at the exact elastic snap. Too early = limp reload; too late = the bubble bursts and you lose. Each clean pop spawns a bigger reward bubble. · trick: near-perfection itch · dare: pops
2. **Story Streak** (story-streak, viral) — _swipe-up stories_ · Swipe up through a deck of story cards; hit the hidden 'next story' rhythm without skipping the mark. The tempo accelerates and the gap shrinks. Miss once and the streak burns like a 24h story. · trick: loss aversion · dare: stories
3. **Like Blink** (like-blink, viral) — _double-tap like_ · Hearts drift across the canvas; double-tap them before they fade. The heart you missed stays ghosted for a beat, clawing at the FOMO of a lost like. · trick: ego bait · dare: likes
4. **FYP Roulette** (fyp-roulette, viral) — _FYP unpredictability_ · A vertical feed of colored tiles flies by. Swipe up to skip, swipe down to save. One random tile is the 'jackpot clip'. The faster the feed, the more the variable reward anticipation screams. · trick: variable reward cliffhanger · dare: jackpots
5. **Pinch to Fit** (pinch-to-fit, viral) — _pinch/zoom_ · A target shape pulses in the center. Pinch to shrink it, spread to grow it, then release when it perfectly frames the ghost outline. The outline flashes for one frame like a thirst trap you almost caught. · trick: near-perfection itch · dare: %
6. **Long Press Loot** (long-press-loot, viral) — _long-press_ · Hold your thumb on a glowing chest. A bar fills from green to red. Release inside the narrow gold zone to bank the loot; hold too long and the chest explodes. Each round the zone shrinks. · trick: loss aversion · dare: gems
7. **Autoplay Chicken** (autoplay-chicken, viral) — _autoplay next_ · A countdown bar sweeps across the bottom like the next-video autoplay timer. Tap to stop it as close to zero as possible without letting the next 'video' start. The screen jitters as if it is about to load. · trick: curiosity gap · dare: ms
8. **Loop Trap** (loop-trap, viral) — _seamless video loops_ · A short animation repeats. A single frame is slightly different each loop. Tap when you spot the glitch before the loop resets. The seamless loop lulls System 2; the glitch hijacks novelty. · trick: participation-comment-bait · dare: loops
9. **Badge Bomb** (badge-bomb, viral) — _red badge compulsion_ · Red notification dots swarm the screen. Tap them in order before they multiply. Tapping the wrong dot spawns three more. The brain cannot ignore a badge; here, clearing it is the game. · trick: shock-honesty · dare: cleared
10. **Duet Trace** (duet-trace, viral) — _duet/remix_ · A ghost hand traces a shape; your thumb must mirror it in real time. The closer the overlap, the higher the 'duet score'. It feels like TikTok stitch muscle memory turned competitive. · trick: competitive rank · dare: %
11. **Filter Shift** (filter-shift, viral) — _filter face-transform_ · A face-like mask morphs through filters. Tap when the filter matches the target prompt (e.g. 'old', 'neon', 'cartoon'). The mask only holds the target for a split second before it slides to the next trend. · trick: ego bait · dare: matches
12. **Infinite Fall** (infinite-fall, viral) — _infinite scroll_ · A tile falls endlessly through a feed of gaps. Swipe left/right to steer it through the open slots. Speed rises the deeper you go, turning the scroll habit into a vertical survival reflex. · trick: competitive rank · dare: floors
13. **Seen Receipt** (seen-receipt, viral) — _seen-receipt anticipation_ · A message bubble pulses; a tiny 'seen' check fades in and out. Tap exactly as the second check appears. Early = desperate; late = ignored. The anxiety of waiting becomes the input cue. · trick: benchmark ego bait · dare: ms
14. **Live Count Climb** (live-count-climb, viral) — _live viewer count_ · A number ticks up and down like a live viewer count. Tap the screen to 'go live' when the count hits a target. The count jitters unpredictably, turning social proof into a twitch-aim game. · trick: ego bait · dare: viewers
15. **Streak Flame** (streak-flame, viral) — _streak anxiety_ · A flame burns at the center. Swipe up to feed it once per beat. Miss a beat and the flame shrinks; three misses and it dies. The streak is visible, growing, and fragile. · trick: loss aversion · dare: days
16. **DM Typing** (dm-typing, viral) — _typing indicator anticipation_ · A three-dot typing indicator bobs. Tap when the dots stop and the message 'sends'. The pause length is random; the compulsion is the same held breath you feel in a chat window. · trick: curiosity gap · dare: msgs
17. **Comment Race** (comment-race, viral) — _comment-section speed_ · Comments fly upward like a live comment section. Tap the ones that match a hidden keyword before they scroll past. The faster the thread, the more the speed-reading brain locks in. · trick: participation-comment-bait · dare: comments
18. **Save Scroll** (save-scroll, viral) — _save/bookmark compulsion_ · Precious items flicker past in a feed. Swipe right to save, left to skip. The catch: you have only three save slots and the best items appear when your slots are full, forcing a painful curation choice. · trick: loss aversion · dare: saves
19. **Profile Grid Match** (profile-grid-match, viral) — _profile grid browsing_ · A 3x3 grid of colored tiles flashes like a profile page. Tap the tile that changed. As rounds speed up, the grid gains 'posts' that distract from the changed one. · trick: play-along comment bait · dare: rounds
20. **Reply Window** (reply-window, viral) — _reply-before-it-disappears_ · A story ring shrinks like an ephemeral reply timer. Type the shown phrase before the ring closes. Each correct reply extends the timer by a hair; each typo burns time. · trick: curiosity gap · dare: replies
21. **Soft Refresh** (soft-refresh, calm) — _pull-to-refresh lever_ · Pull the screen down slowly. The further you pull, the slower your breath is guided. Release inside the calm zone to plant a seed. No score; the feedback is a garden tile that appears once per day. · trick: calm completion
22. **Endless Pond** (endless-pond, calm) — _infinite scroll_ · Scroll a pond surface downward. Lotus pads drift by; catch a petal by tapping, but the pond never ends and never scores. Each petal brightens the water for a moment, then fades. The scroll habit becomes a slow contemplation. · trick: the feed that ends
23. **Double Tap Breath** (double-tap-breath, calm) — _double-tap like_ · A heart gently expands and contracts. Double-tap on the exact exhale beat to sound a soft chime. The heart animation hijacks the like reflex and redirects it into paced breathing. · trick: calm completion
24. **Loop Rest** (loop-rest, calm) — _seamless video loops_ · A slow loop of nature shapes repeats. The instruction is to watch one full loop, then close your eyes for the next. The loop is the cue; the closed-eye pause is the game. No input required. · trick: calm completion
25. **No Badge** (no-badge, calm) — _red badge compulsion_ · Red dots appear. You do not tap them. They fade on their own if left alone. Each un-tapped badge dissolves into a soft ripple. The point is to unlearn the swipe-to-clear reflex. · trick: shock-honesty
